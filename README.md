### A little about me

Maths & Computing student who got way too interested in what data can actually tell you — about markets, systems, people, and the universe itself.

I'm drawn to problems that don't have obvious answers and to building things that make complex ideas easier to understand. Most of my interests sit somewhere at the intersection of software, data, mathematics, finance, and space research.

Currently stuck between writing equations and writing code, with quantitative finance and space technology pulling me in two very different (and somehow related) directions.

When i'm not debugging, you'll probably find me reading about quant strategies, going down a rabbit hole on astrodynamics, or convincing myself that touching grass is a reasonable use of time.

### ✦ Things i've built

**📈 Xiaomi Revenue Analysis**
compared revenue growth and volatility across Xiaomi's smartphone and IoT segments using Python. started as a business case study, ended up teaching me how to think about trends, risk, and what numbers reveal when you stop looking at them as just numbers.
`Python` `Pandas` `Matplotlib` · [repo →](#)

**🌌 Smart Mars Rover**
built an autonomous rover navigation system using the A* pathfinding algorithm to find optimal routes through obstacle-filled terrain. part AI, part graph theory, and a reminder that getting from point A to point B is a lot harder when you're pretending it's Mars.
`C++` `A* Algorithm` `Graph Theory` `Path Planning` · [repo →](#)
